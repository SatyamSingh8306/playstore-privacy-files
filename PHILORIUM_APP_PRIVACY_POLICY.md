## 1. Overview

Philorium is an offline philosophy learning and meditation app. It lets you read
about schools of thought, philosophers, and philosophical stories, and practice
daily meditation with streaks and short notes.

This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is used. **In short:
Philorium does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.** All
data you create stays on your own device.

## 2. Information We Collect

Philorium does **not** require an account, sign-in, or any personal details to
use. We do not collect your name, email, phone number, location, contacts,
photos, or any identifier.

The only data the app stores is data **you create yourself**, and it is saved
**locally on your device only**:

| Data | How it is created | Where it is stored |
| ---- | ---------------- | ----------------- |
| Meditation sessions (date, minutes) | When you complete a meditation timer | Device local storage |
| Meditation streaks | Derived from your session history | Device local storage |
| Daily reflection notes | When you write a note | Device local storage |

This local storage is implemented using the device's own sandbox storage
(AsyncStorage / app-private storage) and is **not** uploaded to any server.

## 3. Sharing of Information

We do **not** share, sell, rent, or transfer any information to third parties.
Because no personal data leaves your device, there is nothing to disclose or
share.

## 4. Analytics, Advertising, and Third-Party SDKs

Philorium uses **no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking
SDKs.** The app contains no in-app purchases and no ads.

## 5. Permissions

Philorium does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts,
location, files, or any other device permission. The meditation timer runs
entirely on-device.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Philorium does not knowingly collect personal information from children under
the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). Because the
app stores no personal data at all, no such information is collected from any
user. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app,
please contact us and we will address it.

## 7. Data Deletion

Since all data lives on your device, you can delete it at any time by:

- Clearing the app's data in your device settings, or
- Uninstalling the app.

This permanently removes your meditation history, streaks, and notes.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted
within the app or on the app's store listing, and the "Effective date" above
will be revised accordingly.
